Brief Description

The home to most (if not all) chambers. Here is where you can access most of the new chambers. This chamber used to be a Tutorial, showing you the controls of the game. Later on, the chamber was revamped to "The Hub" and these controls were removed from the chamber. The Hub consists of 5 categories: Misc, Minigames, Testing, For Fun, and Playground Playground: Only the Main Chamber, for now. For Fun: Chambers made entirely for fun and with the purpose of being fun, yet not minigames. Testing: Made for testing purposes. Minigames: Well, minigames, self explanatory. The Candy Collectors are here. Misc: Chambers that don't fit on other categories are put here. The Playground category doesn't have a sign, despite this, the main chamber counts as a playground chamber.
