Differences between 1.0 and 1.1

1- A Bridge was added in-between the two playable rooms. And due to the inclusion of the bridge, the two rooms were moved from each other. 2- The Pose of the Smirnov Ragdoll is different. 3- A canned drink got added as a prop. 4- Smirnov has a Hat on his head.
