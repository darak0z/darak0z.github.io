1.10

Brief Description

Released on May 30, 2022 (1.10 Alpha 1), September 16th, 2022 (1.10 Alpha 2), November 2nd, 2022(1.10 Full Release) Two new chambers were added, and one was revamped, The Hub and the Main Chamber got an update to their layout, The radio got a bunch of new songs and the Café got a jukebox, Many bugs were fixed and a lot of improvements were added, Photo Mode and Video Settings were added, Might be one of, if not THE biggest update to date.
