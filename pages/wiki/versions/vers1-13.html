1.13

Brief Description

Released on August 13th, 2023 Added a Classes system to HOOT ETERNAL and rewrote Melee weapons Added a lot of cosmetics, and a slight improvement to the Custom Robonov UI, Added miscellaneous additions to the game, And Added "Competitive Mode" in select chambers.

Patch Notes

MAJOR HOOT ETERNAL UPDATE - Added a main menu, with a level select, class select, it's own theme song, and much more. - Added a CLASSES system to HOOT ETERNAL     - Each class has different stats (Health, Speed, Passive Skills, Active Skills). You can see all of this in the HOOT ETERNAL Main Menu.     - Four classes are currently available: The Factory-New, The Agile, The Tank, and The Reckless.     - Selecting a class DOES NOT override your customization options.     - A class quick-switcher is also available in-game by pressing Esc and selecting the desired class in the menu on the top. - The Revolver now has a unique shooting animation and a slight remodel. - The Dynamites now have a unique throwing animation. - Remade the explosion particles and reduced the clutter they caused. - Added an "Autorun" option (Run by default, Walk by holding shift) to the settings menu. Works outside of HOOT too! - Melee Rewrite     - Melee attacks are more consistent now.     - Added Melee damage randomization, like the other weapons.     - In addition to this, Melee attacks now have a 10% chance of being a Crit, dealing 1.35x damage, indicated by a yellow damage number. - Improvements to the HUD     - Shortened "HEALTH" as "HP".     - Changed "TIME" and "KEYS" to icons.     - Removed "WEAPON" text and lowered the sprite to accommodate. - Overhauled the Crosshair, it now represents the accuracy of your weapon rather than simply being a static image. BASE GAME CHANGES - Remade the Main Menu's UI with a much better look and new options. - Added a FOV slider and a "Show Controls" button to the Settings menu. - You can now also crouch by holding C (Ctrl still works)     - To accommodate for this, the Zoom In/Out keys have been swapped from C/Z to X/Z - Added Competitive Mode™ to select chambers     - Available in the Maze levels, and the Block Puzzle     - Tracks your time and saves your best time after beating the level.     - Press [G] to enable Competitive Mode™     - Press [V] to delete your Best Time - New Cosmetics:     - 8 Antenna Shapes     - 26 Hats     - 14 Faces     - 3 Unlockable Cosmetics - New Styles for existing Cosmetics:     - Antenna Shape: Stars (Four Point)     - Face: Angry (Symbol) - Cosmetics with extra styles now have the styles as smaller buttons, to avoid cluttering the UI. - Improved the models of the Lalo and Devilnov NPCs
