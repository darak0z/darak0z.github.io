1.12

Brief Description

Released on May 31st, 2023 Completely remade the Robonov Customization UI Added a lot of new cosmetics (Hats, Faces, Colors, etc) Added Unlockable cosmetics and new styles to existing ones Miscellaneous HOOT ETERNAL improvements (bugfixes, pitch randomization, gibs, etc)

Patch Notes

- Remade the UI for Robonov Customization entirely! - New Color picker, with 16 new colors! - New Hat, Antenna Shape, and Face picker too! - Added a Hat Y offset slider, to modify the position of the hats!     - Added 36 Hats!     - Added 16 Faces!     - Added 6 Antenna Shapes! - Added extra styles to existing cosmetics:     - Hat: Silly Pumpkin (Stem)     - Hat: Sunglasses (Shiny)     - Hat: Traffic Cone (Orange Bottom) - Antenna Shape: Spikes (Dark) - Added unlockable cosmetics!     - NOTE: Some older cosmetics have been converted to unlockable ones. - Added an Emote menu! Open it by pressing B.     - NOTE: Emotes are only visible in Photo Mode or through a mirror. - Added 1 Radio song. - HOOT ETERNAL Changes:     - Reverted the 1.11 Machete change. It does not attack multiple enemies at once anymore. To compensate, damage has been increased by 4 (35 -> 39).     - Increased the Fire Rate of the Lever Action Sniper by 0.02s (1.15s -> 1.13s)     - Shotgun damage is now cumulative (It won't show 5 damage numbers anymore, instead it will only show one, with the total amount of damage done)     - Fixed the screen blood effect not properly working if the player was damaged by explosives     - Added death reasons, to know what killed you.     - Decreased the Double Shotgun spread/inaccuracy by 0.08 (2.8 -> 2.72)     - You now explode into multiple pieces upon dying!     - Remade the Muzzle Flash particle and Bullet Hole texture     - Added pitch randomization to gun shooting sound effects
