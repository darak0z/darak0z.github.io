1.15

Brief Description

Released on September 22nd, 2024 Robonov is now taller and his eyes line up with the camera. Added only a few new cosmetics. Most of them for the 5th anniversary. Optimized and heavily rebalanced HOOT ETERNAL, along with a rewrite of the weapon system.

Patch Notes

GENERAL

- Made the player 10% taller - Properly lined up Robonov’s eyes with the camera. - Properly lined up the camera position when crouching and increased crouching height. - Increased maximum FOV to 130 (previously 110) - New Cosmetics:     - 1 Hat, with 2 styles     - 2 free Hats for the game’s 5th anniversary (with a extra style each)

HOOT ETERNAL

- Optimized a lot of code. - Rewrote weapons completely. - You can now pick up and drop weapons manually. (Press G to drop) - Improved weapon animations. - Added switching weapons by using the scroll wheel. - Fixed enemies sliding after dying. - Improved bullet holes. - Shotguns now have a 100% accurate first pellet. - Added Input Buffering (You can spam click without losing inputs/shots) - Added enemy weak spots (Shoot their head to deal extra damage) - Weapons are now visible in Photo Mode!     - Some emotes are compatible with your weapons too, meaning they don’t hide them when emoting. - Damage values are now internally decimal (damage numbers are visually rounded) - Weapon Rebalalancing:     - Machete: Increased damage. Reduced fire rate. New attack animation.     - Revolver: Increased damage and fire rate. Reduced spread.     - Double Shotgun: Lightly reduced spread. Added 100% accurate pellet.     - Drum Gun: Lightly reduced spread. Increased damage.     - Lever Action Sniper: Damage rebalanced. Increased fire rate.     - Dynamites: Decreased self-damage. - Class Rebalancing:     - All: Damage buff passive skills are now multipliers, and not a flat number.     - Thorn: Increased Dual Drum Guns damage.     - Brutus: Complete active skill rework. Increased movement speed.     - Bash: Increased Shark Mine detection range and reduced fuse time.     - Nano: Reduced Sniper ammo regen. General buffs to the Hexastopper. You now cannot roll the same Trusty gun twice in a row.
