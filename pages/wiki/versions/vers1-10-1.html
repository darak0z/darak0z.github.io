1.10.1

Brief Description

Released on November 19th, 2022 HOOT ETERNAL Rebalancing and Discord RPC issue fixing. A small update, but pretty significant to HOOT ETERNAL's gameplay.

Patch Notes

- Despite it not being Halloween anymore, the decorations will remain until the next major update (1.11). - Fixed some Discord Rich Presence issues (Missing images, capitalization, and spelling) - Added more loading screen tips. - Changed the Restart Chamber keybind in HOOT ETERNAL to "K" to avoid accidentally restarting because you tried to reload your gun (You can't reload, it's automatic). - Added a Completion Badge to HOOT ETERNAL: TIME TRIAL I upon getting all kills. - Re-rendered HOOT ETERNAL: TIME TRIAL I's chamber preview and Discord RPC image. - Rebalanced HOOT ETERNAL: TIME TRIAL I.     - Increased amount of time available (3 minutes -> 5 minutes)     - Reduced enemy count by 6 (66 -> 60)     - Decreased Revolver spread/inaccuracy by 0.15 (0.7 -> 0.55)     - Increased Drum Gun range by 5 (65 -> 70)     - Decreased Drum Gun spread/inaccuracy by 0.1 (1.35 -> 1.25)     - Increased the HP of the Bombardier by 20 (300 -> 320)     - Decreased Swordsmen base damage by 1 (28 -> 27)     - Decreased Miniswordsmen base damage by 2 (23 -> 21)     - Enemies get stunned for 0.13 seconds if their pain chance triggers (In other words: If they're hit and you're lucky).     - Added more Ammo Crates.     - Added more Medkits
