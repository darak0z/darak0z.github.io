1.9

Brief Description

Released on October 31st, 2021. The second ever Halloween update of the game. It added a new Candy Collector level, The Graveyard. It also added many other improvements and additions to the game.

Patch Notes

-There's a new Candy Collector level!    °Level 2, The Graveyard -Various bug fixes    °Fixed the weird shadow the campfire projected onto the ceiling on the Main Chamber -Removed some unnecessary and unused stuff    °Removed Lighting Test Chamber as its literally useless -Added a collision/impact sound effect to all props, and also the ragdoll in the main chamber (yes, like in Gmod or Half Life 2) -Added an NPC Hangout room to the Main Chamber.    °There's new characters that you can talk to! -Also added NPCs to other chambers -Added a VHS Room to the Main Chamber.    °Put the VHS in the VHS Player to play videos on the TV! -Game should run better as some scripts were merged and optimized. -Added more songs to the Vinyl Player in the Main Chamber! °New Special song, "Sugar Overload" by lau & me!    °There's also a hidden Vinyl somewhere. -The Candy Collector's UI was changed.    °The Title Card now shows the NAME of the level instead of just saying if its level 1, 2, etc    °The Candy Counter now shows a sprite of a Candy instead of simply a text saying "Candies".    °The Minimap was removed    °Added a text that shows the amount of candies you need to get -Added a sound effect for when you win in the Candy Collector levels. -Added an easy way to quit the game, simply press ESC to instantly quit. -Added a Loading Screen so that going to other levels isn't just a game freeze and a teleport. -Added an extra Dialogue to The Plant in The Café if you move his drink away from him. -Added more ragdolls to the Main Chamber, because why not. -Candy Collector Levels have a new, scarier song!    °The old song is now the Candy Collector Hub song -Made the Block Puzzle tips more helpful. -The Big Blocks in the Hub now rotate, to give it more life.
